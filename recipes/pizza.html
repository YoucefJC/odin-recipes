Pizza Recipe

Description

This margherita pizza is knocked up with fantastic homemade dough, sweet tomato, beautiful creamy mozzarella and fragrant basil. What could be more delicious!

Ingredients

  • Warm water (250ml)
  • Salt (3g)
  • Flour 00 (450g)
  • Bread yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix the salt in the water
  2. Mix the yeast with the water
  3. Add the flour
  4. Kneed for 10min
  5. Let it rest for 1 hour or overnight in the fridge
  6. Take the dough and shape it with your hands
  7. Add a little bit of tomato sauce
  8. Top with mozzarella and basil leaves
  9. Drizzle with a little bit of olive oil
  10. Put in a pre heated oven (250Â°C) for about 8 minutes at the bottom
